How it works
The Process
1. Provide Data

Start by submitting the data you need analyzed. Our secure and user-friendly platform makes it easy for you to upload your dataset in the format that suits your research.

2. Share Requirements

Along with your data, share a detailed requirements document. Describe the analysis goals, specific mathematical concepts, and any tests you want conducted.

3. Collab on Analysis

We believe in a collaborative approach. As we analyze your data, you're welcome to sit in on sessions or discuss progress with our data analysts.

4. Get results

Receive a thorough analysis tailored to your exact specifications, empowering you to move forward with confidence. You can focus on drawing conclusions and advancing your research.

Our Limitations
What We Cannot Do
Design Your Research
The data analyst will not be responsible for designing the research. It is the researcher's responsibility to know what they want to achieve with the analysis.
Conduct the Research
DARANDA is not responsible for conducting the research itself, only for performing the data analysis based on the provided requirements.
Automatic Implementation of Suggestions
Data analysts may offer suggestions, but they will only be implemented if the researcher agrees to them.
Go against university norms
We would not like to violate university norms on research. We suggest you ask your research guides or professors if you can work with us.
